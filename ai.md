Right now, we are experiencing an inflection period that will shape the nature of humanity and society for generations.
There are several questions we must address as we progress through the third-stage of computing in the fourth industrial revolution.
This period of rapid technology evolution and expansion embodies the most significant global transformation of the digital age.
Artificial intelligence technology will serve as a cocktail composed of the trifecta of change agents: it will act as ***a disruptor***, ***an amplifier***, and ***an accelerant***.

This article mini-series examines several questions through the lenses of computer engineering and philosophy;
the intent is to stimulate curiosity by providing concrete examples and posing thought experiments for consideration.
We must find our path forward and take advantage of this narrow window to alter our current trajectory.
